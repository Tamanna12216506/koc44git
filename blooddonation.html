donation process


1.first click on register and enroll your profile on the website
2.then your login id and password will be sent to you in form a message
3.then come back to home page and enter your login id and password.and click on login
4.then select the place where you would like to donate your blood.
5.you will get a call from our representative of your selected blood bank.
6.our representative will send you an sms in which you come to know the date and time of donation of your blood.
7.and you will also get health instructions from our medical expert.
